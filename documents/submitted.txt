Artificial intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by computer systems.
Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables systems to learn from data.
Deep learning uses neural networks with many layers to analyze various factors of data.
Natural language processing allows computers to understand and interpret human language.
Reinforcement learning is a type of machine learning where an agent learns by interacting with an environment.
Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services over the internet, including servers, storage, and databases.
Data science is an interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods to extract knowledge from data.
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks.
Blockchain technology is a system of recording information in a way that makes it impossible to change or hack.
Algorithms are step-by-step procedures or formulas for solving problems or accomplishing tasks.
Data structures are ways of organizing and storing data in a computer so it can be accessed and modified efficiently.
Object-oriented programming is a programming paradigm based on the concept of objects.
Software development is the process of designing, coding, testing, and maintaining software applications.
The Internet of Things connects physical devices to the internet, enabling data collection and exchange.
Augmented reality enhances real-world environments with computer-generated perceptual information.
